Web Development

Developer & Designer

Web developers design and build websites. They are typically responsible for the appearance of the site and technical aspects, such as site speed and how much traffic the site can handle. Web developers may also create site content that requires technical features.

I have been creating websites since 2021, I like to build bold and minimalist websites that can cause a positive impact for the brand/company.

Website and Software application designing, building an mainteinance, using scripting or authoring languages, managment tools, content creation tools, applications and digital media, conferring with teams to resolve conflicts, prioritize needs, develop content criteria, or choose solutions.

I love seeing an intricate application function seamlessly
When designing an application, conceptualizing the technical flow can make your head hurt, and you wonder how you will ever make something so complicated actually come together. When you have such a tough task, you have no choice but to break it up into smaller pieces. After the long hours and the application finally comes together, there’s nothing better than the “I can’t believe this actually works” feeling.

I love that my work can speak for itself
I prefer talking to my computer more than I like talking to people. That’s not to say talking to a computer is all a web developer needs to do – quite the contrary. However, I appreciate that as a web developer, my value is shown by my work much more than social buttering. Once I have created an amazing application, I just let my app do the talking.
